### Short Bio
Andrea Terlizzi is a PhD student at the University of Salerno and a member of the NeuroneLab since 2023, having initially joined as a research assistant during his MSc. His research focuses on NeuroAI, developing brain-inspired deep learning models, connectome-constrained neural networks, reservoir computing, and dynamic or conditional computation approaches for biologically informed artificial intelligence.
