### Short Bio
Angelo Nazzaro is an MSc student in Machine Learning at the University of Salerno and a member of the NeuRoNeLab since 2023, having joined the lab after completing his BSc thesis in Computer Science there. His research focuses on applying deep learning to remote sensing data as well as to neuroimaging and computational neuroscience applications.
