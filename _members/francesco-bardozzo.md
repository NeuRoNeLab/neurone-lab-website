### Short Bio
Francesco Bardozzo is an assistant professor at the University of Salerno and a member of the NeuroneLab research group, where he studies the intersection of artificial intelligence, connectomics, and computational biology. His research explores explainable and bio-inspired AI, particularly connectome-constrained models, and deep learning applications in fields like computational biology, medical imaging, and generative music.
