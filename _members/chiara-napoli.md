### Short Bio
Chiara Napoli is a PhD student at Sapienza University of Rome and a member of the NeuroneLab research group since 2024. Her research focuses on multi-omic data analysis and clinical data integration, with applications in single-cell data analysis and the development of computational approaches for combining diverse biological and clinical datasets.
